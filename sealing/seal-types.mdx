SealMyIdea offers three core seal types, available both in the web app and through the SealAPI.

## Trusted Timestamp (e-seal)

An organization-level seal that proves **integrity and time**: the document existed at the sealed moment and has not changed since. No personal signature is involved.

- **Synchronous**: the sealed PDF is returned immediately
- A qualified electronic timestamp is applied via Swisscom Trust Services
- A visual signature box and QR code are stamped on the PDF
- Consumes folder-sealing or timestamp credits

Best for: protecting ideas, drafts, designs, and any document where you need court-ready proof of existence rather than a personal signature.

API: [Timestamp seal a PDF](/api-reference/sealing/timestamp-seal-a-pdf)

## On-Demand qualified electronic signature (QES)

A personal **qualified electronic signature** issued on demand through Swisscom, without prior Mobile ID registration. The signer provides their name and verified mobile number (MSISDN), then approves the signature on their phone.

- **Asynchronous**: the signing request goes to the signer's phone; poll the document status until it completes
- Produces a QES that is legally equivalent to a handwritten signature (eIDAS for the EU, ZertES for Switzerland)
- Consumes e-Signature credits for the selected regulation

Best for: single-signer agreements where the signer may not have a registered Swisscom Mobile ID.

## MAB multi-signer signing

MAB (Multiple Authentication Broker) collects qualified signatures from **one or many people**. Each signer approves the document from their phone via the Swisscom signing app.

- Up to **20 co-signers** besides the owner; co-signers are invited by email and sign in sequence
- The owner can choose to sign first or last, revoke a pending signer, or stop reminder emails
- Requires an active MAB subscription; 1 credit is used per signer, per that signer's regulation
- **Asynchronous with a redirect flow**: you receive a consent URL for the owner, and each signer returns to your redirect URL after signing

Best for: contracts and certificates that several stakeholders must sign.

API: [MAB multi-signer signing](/api-reference/sealing/mab-multi-signer-signing) and the [MAB flow guide](/guides/mab-flow)

## Comparison

| | Trusted Timestamp | On-Demand QES | MAB multi-signer |
|---|---|---|---|
| Proves | Integrity + time | Personal signature | Personal signatures (1 to 21 people) |
| Signature level | Qualified e-seal / timestamp | Qualified electronic signature | Qualified electronic signature |
| Processing | Synchronous | Asynchronous (phone approval) | Asynchronous (consent URL + email invites) |
| Mobile ID registration needed | No | No (on-demand identity) | Yes (Swisscom Mobile ID) |
| Credits | Timestamp / folder credits | e-Signature credits | 1 credit per signer |
| Regulations | ZertES, eIDAS | ZertES, eIDAS | ZertES, eIDAS |

## More sealing surfaces

Beyond the three core types:

- **[Folder sealing](/sealing/folder-sealing)**: seal many files, or many versions of one document, together under a single folder with a proof certificate.
- **[Certificate sealing](/sealing/certificate-sealing)**: create certificates from a PDF or template, position a QR overlay, optionally collect signatures, then seal. Supports extra signature variants including hand-signing.
- **UAE Pass sealing**: for accounts signed in with UAE Pass, documents can be sealed with a UAE Pass digital signature (PAdES or CAdES). Available in the web app for UAE Pass accounts.
