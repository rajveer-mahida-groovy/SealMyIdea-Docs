## What is SealMyIdea?

SealMyIdea is a Swiss trust platform for protecting documents and ideas. It applies a cryptographic, legally recognized seal to your PDF so that its content, and the exact time it existed, are provably fixed and tamper-evident.

Every seal is created through **Swisscom Trust Services**, a qualified trust service provider (QTSP), and complies with:

- **ZertES**, the Swiss federal law on electronic signatures
- **eIDAS**, the European Union regulation on electronic identification and trust services

A sealed document carries an embedded PAdES digital signature, a qualified timestamp, a SHA-256 content hash, and a QR code that anyone can scan to verify the document's authenticity. Sealing is irreversible: once sealed, a document is permanently timestamped and any later modification is detectable.

## What can you do with it?

- **Timestamp sealing**: apply a qualified electronic timestamp (organization e-seal) to any PDF in a single synchronous API call
- **Qualified electronic signatures (QES)**: collect personal signatures that are legally equivalent to handwritten ones, approved by the signer on their phone via Swisscom Mobile ID
- **Multi-signer workflows**: up to 20 co-signers per document, invited by email and signing in sequence
- **Folder sealing**: keep multiple sealed versions of one document grouped in a folder, with a proof certificate and a trusted version history
- **Verification**: anyone can verify a sealed document via its QR code or the public document validator, with no account needed
